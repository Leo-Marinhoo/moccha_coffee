ABOUT US

Since 1989, Moccha Cafe has been serving not just as a meeting point for coffee lovers but also as a symbol of excellence in coffee beans. Founded with the vision of offering a unique coffee experience, Moccha is more than a cafe, it’s a journey through the flavor and aroma of the most exquisite beans. Our passion for coffee is evident in every cup. We cultivate our own premium beans on fertile lands, where each bean is treated with the utmost care and attention. The result is a line of distinctive coffees, each with its own story and flavor profile that you won’t find anywhere else.

DISTRIBUTION LOCATIONS

Moccha, the coffee brand cherished by connoisseurs worldwide, is proud to announce the expansion of our distribution network. Our aromatic and richly flavored coffee beans are now reaching more corners of the globe than ever before. From the bustling city streets of New York to the serene landscapes of Kyoto, Moccha is there to invigorate your senses. In Europe, our presence has grown in the romantic cafes of Paris, the historic squares of Rome, and the modern coffee houses of Berlin. Down under, Australians can enjoy our brews in Sydney’s vibrant coffee scene. In South America, we’ve made our mark in the coffee-loving cities of Bogotá and São Paulo. And let’s not forget the vibrant markets of Marrakech and Cape Town in Africa, where Moccha is fast becoming a household name.
